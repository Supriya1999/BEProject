About Us

KJEI’s Trinity College of Engineering and Research was established June 2008 at the scenic foothills of Bopdev Ghat, Yewlewadi, Pisoli,Pune. The College is approved by AICTE & DTE,Govt of Maharashtra and is Affiliated to Savitribai Phule Pune University. The college boasts of quality state of art infrastructure with well planned,designed adequate class rooms and labs. The College is in the forefront to impart quality education & to ultimately be recognized as a premiere knowledge centre.
